# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

### Acceptable Behavior

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards others

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Hate speech or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks or insults
- Harassment or intimidation
- Publishing private information without consent
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior

## Enforcement

Community leaders will follow these guidelines:

1. **Warning**: First offense or minor violation
2. **Temporary restriction**: Repeated violations
3. **Permanent ban**: Severe or continued violations

## Reporting

Report violations by:
- Creating an issue with the `moderation` label
- Contacting repository maintainers directly
